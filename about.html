About Sammie

Who's Mentally Got Y'all!

Sammie LLC (Sammie) was founded two years ago by Samantha Ryan Bagenda, a Ugandan IT specialist and psychologist. Samantha has in-depth knowledge in Clinical Psychology and Information Technology, which also happen to be her areas of expertise.

With her blend of tech and psychology, her work revolves around being friendly, disciplined and organized yet flexible. With her high advent high technology, she's helping patients resolve their emotional issues seamlessly.
